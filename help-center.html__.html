Getting Started

Welcome to DreamGate! This guide will walk you through the main features of our app to help you get started on your journey of dream exploration.

  • The Interpreter: Simply type your dream into the text box on the Interpreter page. Provide as much detail as possible for the best results. Select a focus and category, then click "Interpret My Dream."
  • The Dictionary: Use the Dictionary page to search for specific symbols you remember from your dream. This is a great way to learn about individual elements.
  • The Journal: After creating an account, you can save your interpretations to your personal Dream Journal. This allows you to track patterns and recurring themes over time.

Using the AI Interpreter

Our AI is designed to be a compassionate guide. Here are some tips for getting the most insightful interpretations:

  • Be Detailed: Include feelings, colors, locations, people, and any strange or unusual events.
  • Use the "Focus Type": Select a focus like "Psychological" or "Spiritual" to guide the AI's perspective and get an interpretation that aligns with your interests.
  • Review and Reflect: The interpretation is a starting point. Use the reflection questions to explore how the dream's message might apply to your waking life.
