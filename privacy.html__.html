Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

Welcome to DreamGate. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: This includes the text of the dreams you submit for interpretation, your email address if you create an account, and any feedback you send us.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our service, such as which features you use and the frequency of your visits, to help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: The dream text you submit is sent to our AI provider (Google Gemini) solely to generate your interpretation. We do not use it for any other purpose.
  • To Manage Your Account: If you create an account, your email is used for login, password resets, and important service announcements.
  • To Improve Our Service: Anonymous usage data helps us understand what is working and what isn't, so we can make DreamGate better.

3. Data Storage and Security

Interpretations: Dreams submitted for interpretation are processed by the AI and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save the interpretation to your Dream Journal.

Dream Journal: If you use the Dream Journal feature, your saved dreams and interpretations are stored securely in our database (Supabase). This data is encrypted and accessible only to you through your account.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not and will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your anonymous dream text is shared with our AI provider for the sole purpose of generating an interpretation and is subject to their privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you have a Dream Journal account, you can manage your saved entries directly within the app. To request the deletion of your entire account and all associated data, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@dreamgate.com.
