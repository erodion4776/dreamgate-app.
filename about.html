About DreamGate

We help you understand your dreams with compassionate, AI-powered insight.

Our Mission

DreamGate exists to make self-understanding accessible. We combine psychology, symbolism, cultural context and spiritual perspectives to provide compassionate, practical dream interpretations that help you reflect, heal, and grow.

What we do

  • Provide AI-assisted dream interpretations that are empathetic and practical.
  • Offer a private dream journal to track personal patterns over time.
  • Provide resources to explore symbolism and emotional meaning.

Our values

Compassion, privacy, clarity and user empowerment. Our AI is a tool to support your personal reflection — not a substitute for professional advice.
