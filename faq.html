Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI interpretation work?

Our AI uses an advanced large language model (Google's Gemini) that has been trained on a vast library of psychological texts, cultural symbolism, and mythological archetypes. When you submit a dream, the AI analyzes the text to identify key symbols, themes, and emotions. It then synthesizes this information to provide a multi-layered interpretation based on your selected focus (e.g., psychological, spiritual). This process is designed to provide a comprehensive yet easy-to-understand analysis of your dream's potential meanings.

Is my dream data private and secure?

Absolutely. Privacy is our top priority. The dreams you submit for interpretation are sent securely to the AI for analysis and are not stored by us unless you explicitly choose to save an interpretation to your private, encrypted Dream Journal. We will never share your dream data with third parties. All journal entries are protected and only accessible by you when you are logged into your account.

What is the difference between the subscription plans?

The "Free Explorer" plan allows you a limited number of interpretations per month, giving you a chance to experience the core functionality. The "Dream Master" plan offers unlimited interpretations, access to the personal Dream Journal to track patterns over time, and more advanced AI insights that delve deeper into the psychological and emotional layers of your dreams. You can find more details on our homepage.

Can the AI predict the future?

Our AI is a tool for self-reflection, not a fortune teller. While some traditions believe in prophetic dreams, our interpretations focus on the psychological and symbolic meaning of your dream content as it relates to your current life, emotions, and subconscious thoughts. It helps you understand the "now," not predict the "what if." The goal is to provide insight that you can use for personal growth today.
